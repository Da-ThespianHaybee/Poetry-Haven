About the Poet

I am Abubakar Saadu (Prince Haybee), a poet and truth-seeker dedicated to using words as a force for justice, healing, and change. My poetry explores the struggles and triumphs of humanity — from the plight of the common people to reflections on inner battles and faith.

Poetry-Haven was born from the belief that poetry can be both a refuge and a revolution. This space is a home for truth-telling, emotional honesty, and soul-searching. Each poem here speaks to a deeper part of life, calling readers to reflect, feel, and rise.

I write not just for beauty, but for impact — hoping each verse echoes long after the last line.

— Prince Haybee
